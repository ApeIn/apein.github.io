KissMyMoon Protocol

Auto LP


The fee is split 50/50 half of which is sold to $BNB, while the other half of KissMyMoon is paired automatically. The liquidity fee is then added to the liquidity pool on PancakeSwap (KissMyMoon/BNB) to promote price stability.

Auto Burn


The auto-burn fee depends on the trade size and the type of transaction.

Txn Vol Buying Fee Selling Fee
< 1M 5% 1%
< 10M 5% 2%
< 30M 5% 5%
< 100M 5% 10%

Auto Reward


The reward fee is redistributed to all existing holders. The uniform reward fee acts as another layer of anti-dumping to alleviate sell pressure from crazy APY in DeFi farming.

FAQ

How can investors make sure that KissMyMoon is not scam?
We plan to launch the ILO on Unicrypt, which automatically locks up the liquidity for 12 months, preventing rug-pull. We also applied 24-hour time-lock to our smart contract for functions with ownership privilege. Moreover, there will be governing procedure, and the community will be able to monitor in respect of transparency. Moreover, any changes to the smart contract will be announced to the public immediately. One of our core value is transparency, which represents what we believe in. Besides, KissMyMoon is audited by Solidity Finance to build trust and confidence in our community. The audit report can be found on the website: https://solidity.finance/audits/KissMyMoon/
Is KissMyMoon the the Safemoon fork?
A part of our code is the Safemoon fork. However, we reviewed comments from Certik intensively and refactored the code to the better and more secure version. We also implemented new functions such anti-whale tier system, and deflationary. The smart contract is published on bscscan here: https://bscscan.com/address/0xe577c150456f7754bdbeb25487d0f95d131f0cb8#code
